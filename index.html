About S&S Electricals

Our Story

Founded in 2015, S&S Electrical Works aims at providing single window solution to all electrical infrastructural requirements of Builders & Developers, Banks & Corporates, Government Departments.

We are committed to our clients with a customer-oriented strategy, focusing on innovative and customized products and services. We believe in building cooperative business environment to extract the benefits of the value chain.

Our Mission

To deliver superior values to our customers, stakeholders, employees and society at large. One of the strongest motivating forces at S&S Electrical Works is its 'satisfied customers'.

Our Vision

We want to change the competitive landscape by taking quality to a whole new level, making our services so valuable that they become our clients' only real value proposition.

Our Motto

"PLAN TO DO BETTER, THEN ALWAYS DO BETTER, THAN YOU'D PLANNED."
